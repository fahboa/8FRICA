FAQ

NFT stands for “Non-fungible token” – it is a unique token that exists on a blockchain and cannot be replicated.

NFTs can be used to represent real-world items like artwork and real-estate. "Tokenizing" these real-world tangible assets allows them to be bought, sold and traded more efficiently while reducing the probability of fraud.

NFTs can also be used to represent people’s identities, property rights, membership access (passes), real estate in the metaverse, gaming assets, voting assets within DAOs and much more.

Some NFT’s main function is to be digital art and its value lies entirely in aesthetics.

NFTs can exist on crosschains – ethereum, binance, solana, flow, cardano, tezos are all doing nfts – however we will be working with ethereum as it is the current market leader.

MetaMask is a WEB3 cryptocurrency wallet that can store your compatible tokens and is needed to purchase and mint your NFTs. Having a wallet gives you an Ethereum address (i.e. 0xABCD….1234), this is where your tokens and NFTs will be stored. Learn more about MetaMask and how easy it is to use over here! (https://metamask.io/)

Every transaction on Ethereum costs Gas and the amount of Ether is paid to the miner for processing the transaction. These costs are dependent on the Ethereum network usage or congestion at the time you process the minting of your 8SIAN NFT. At the time of purchase we advise that you have more Ethereum in your wallet on top of the 0.088 required for the 8asian NFT to account for these fees.
